Shipping Guide

How to ship a cutting
safely across Canada.

Canada Post Expedited is your carrier. Domestic leaf and stem cuttings without roots require no CFIA paperwork. Here's the exact method used by every successful plant seller in Canada.

The Method — Step by Step
1. Water the mother plant the day before shipping so the cutting stays hydrated.
2. Cut the stem the morning you ship. Fresh cut survives longest in transit.
3. Wrap the cut end in damp paper towel or sphagnum moss. Seal with tape.
4. Put in a ziplock bag — leave it 25% open so there's air circulation.
5. Cushion with crumpled paper in a small cardboard box. Snug fit, no movement.
6. Label clearly: LIVE PLANTS — FRAGILE — THIS SIDE UP. Ship Monday or Tuesday only.
